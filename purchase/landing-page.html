As a not for profit charitable organization, Frank’s Foundation relies on the generosity of individuals, foundations, corporations and community groups to help us provide our summer camping program to children with illnesses who are apart of Frank’s Foundation.

There are many ways you can make a gift to support Frank’s Foundation Summer Camp:

  • A one time or monthly donation
  • Apply to receive a matching grant from your workplace
  • Give an in kind gift
  • Send a child to camp

All donations are tax deductible and receipts will be issued for all contributions of $10 or more.
